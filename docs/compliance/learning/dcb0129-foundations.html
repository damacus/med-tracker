Internal learning

DCB0129 first

This page explains the first compliance foundation pass for MedTracker. It is designed for learning, not for public assurance or certification.

DCB0129

The software-maker side: intended use, hazards, controls, evidence, gaps, safety case, and incident learning.

DCB0160

The deployment side: a care organisation's local workflows, users, training, rollout, configuration, and residual risk.

ISO 13485

A medical-device quality management system. Deferred until MedTracker has a reason to pursue medical-device QMS work.

SaMD

Software with its own medical purpose. MedTracker is not claiming that boundary in this pass.

What we are building

1. Intended use
What MedTracker does and does not do.
2. Evidence map
Where current code and docs support safety claims.
3. Gap analysis
What is missing before broader users.
4. Hazard log
How things could cause harm and what controls exist.
5. Safety case
A structured argument, not an approval claim.

What we are not claiming

This work does not claim NHS approval, clinical approval, DCB0129 completion, DCB0160 completion, ISO 13485 certification, MHRA registration, UKCA marking, or medical-device status.
