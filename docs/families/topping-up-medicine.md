# Top Up a Medicine by Scanning

When you bring home a new box or bottle of a medicine that is already in
MedTracker, scanning is the quickest way to top it up.

This keeps the medicine record in one place and updates the stock count without
creating a duplicate.

## 1. Open Medications

Sign in to MedTracker and open **Medications** from the navigation.

Use **Scan stock**. This opens a scanner and a short form for the new pack.

## 2. Scan the new pack

Choose **Start Scanner** and point your camera at the barcode on the medicine
box or bottle.

If your camera is not available, enter the barcode manually.

MedTracker will show a match when the barcode belongs to a medicine already in
your inventory. Check that the matched medicine and location look right.

## 3. Enter the quantity

Enter how much stock you are adding from the new pack. Use the same kind of
quantity your household already tracks for that medicine, such as tablets,
capsules, doses, or millilitres.

Choose **Add scanned stock**. MedTracker adds the quantity to the existing
medicine and keeps you on the same inventory record.

## If no match appears

If MedTracker says no medication matched that barcode, the medicine may not have
been added yet or the barcode may be different from the saved one.

Use [Add your first medicine](adding-first-medicine.md) to add it as a new
medicine, or ask the person who manages your MedTracker setup to check the
inventory.
