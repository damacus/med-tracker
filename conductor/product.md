# Initial Concept
Parents recording their own and their childs medication schedule

# Product Guide

## Target Users
- **Parents:** Managing medication schedules for themselves and their children.
- **Family Units:** Requiring a shared view of health-related tasks.

## Goals
- **Safety:** Prevent accidental overdoses by tracking daily limits and time since the last dose.
- **Auditability:** Maintain a clear record of which carer administered which dose and at what time.
- **Schedule Management:** Simplify the coordination of multiple medication schedules (e.g., child's antibiotics and parent's maintenance meds) in one place.

## Key Features (MVP)
- **Unified Family Dashboard:** A single view showing upcoming, taken, and missed doses for all family members.
- **Simple Dose Recording:** An intuitive interface for quickly logging doses as they are administered.

## Security & Privacy
- **Strict Data Privacy:** Ensuring all medical information is handled with the highest standards of security and confidentiality.
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Restricting access to sensitive data so only authorized carers can view or record doses for specific family members.
- **Comprehensive Audit Trail:** Logging every change or entry to ensure safety and accountability within the care team.
